Follower

Follower noun

/ˈfɒləʊə(r)/
is a person who...

1. Supports and admires a particular person or set of ideas

the followers of Mahatma Gandhi

2. Is very interested in a particular activity and follows all the recent news about it

keen followers of football

3. Does things after somebody else has done them first

She is a leader, not a follower.

4.​ Chooses to regularly receive somebody's messages using a social media service

a celebrity with thousands of followers on Twitter
