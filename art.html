A New Normal

Created with Adobe Illustrator, Circa 2020
by Rachel Berlow Josepho

Design for isolation - futurist dystopia.

The word "lockdown" will forever remind us of the fear of the unknown and forced isolation we experienced through the chaos... but what if lockdown was a new normal? What if we never go back to shaking hands or seeing eachother smile? What if we grow towards isolation? What then?

This digital art piece was created during the global crisis of 2020. The backdrop is composed of 1s and 0s to represent humanity's merge with technology. We each live within our own bubble of safety.

Displayed is our "new normal".
