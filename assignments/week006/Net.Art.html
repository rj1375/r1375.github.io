Follower
"Don't go unnoticed. Follower is a service that grants you a real life Follower for a day. A no-hassle unseen companion, someone that watches, someone that sees you, someone who cares."
After you applied and get selected as a follower, you will be given the app to download. You don't have an idea of when it will happen. The following will last one day. At the end, only a photo of you taken by the follower is left.
